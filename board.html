Chitavi Maulloo

I am Chitavi Maulloo (15’), and currently, the publicity chair/ social network liaison/ historian on the Renaissance leadership board. I have a passion for Chemistry, and for understanding how tumors work from a biochemical perspective so I can design nanoparticle agents to try to kill them. I have been exploring my interests through research at the University of Rochester Medical Center since my freshman year, which I hope to pursue through a doctoral degree. When I am not studying, I can be found ballroom dancing, volunteering with former refugee students in the Rochester community, watching a play at Todd Theater, trying to get artistic shots of nature, or more recently, baking. I am happy I get to practice my best at nature photography whenever I visit home, an exquisite sub-tropical island, Mauritius.
