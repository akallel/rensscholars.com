Vision

The Renaissance and Global scholars is a team of motivated world class leaders who strive towards making a positive difference in society.


Mission

  • Build this motivated group of scholars into a united team of leaders.
  • Aid in the growth of our community on and off campus.
  • Give back to the university community through sharing one's experiences, participating in inclusive service work and involvement in intellectually stimulating programs.
  • Lead our scholars by being people of integrity and leaders on campus
